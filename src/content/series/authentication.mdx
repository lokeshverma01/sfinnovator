This series works through **every authentication option Salesforce gives you**, one
provider at a time. For each, we cover what it is, when to reach for it, the trade-offs,
and a complete implementation you can follow in your own org.

Whether you're connecting an external app, enabling SSO for your users, or wiring up a
server-to-server integration, there's a part here for it. Work through it in order, or
jump to the provider you need.
